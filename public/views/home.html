Book Cab

BookCab is a car rental aggregator service based out of New Delhi. It is started in 2017 with metro cities. Its other services include local cab rides and airport ferry. In April 2017, BookCab will start its services in Tier-II and Tier-III cities across the country including Haridwar, Ujjain, Rishikesh, Ooty, Panipat, Meerut, and Ambala. The company is operating in 120 cities with 8,000 vehicles.
